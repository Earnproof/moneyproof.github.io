💬 What is AI Data Labeling?

Data labeling is the process of marking or annotating data to help train AI and machine learning models. Companies need humans to label images, text, audio, and video so that AI algorithms can learn to recognize patterns.

Think of it this way: AI models need to "see" thousands of labeled examples to understand what they're looking at. Your job is to help teach these AI models by correctly labeling data.

Simple Example: If an AI company is building a self-driving car AI, they need millions of road images labeled with "pedestrian," "car," "traffic light," etc. You get paid to identify and mark these objects!

📝 Step-by-Step Getting Started

Step 1: Choose Your Platform

Start with Remotasks (easiest approval) or Appen (most tasks). Create an account and fill out your profile completely.

Each platform requires you to complete 10-50 test tasks perfectly to prove you understand the labeling rules. This usually takes 1-3 hours.

Step 3: Wait for Approval

Once approved, you get access to available projects. This takes 1-7 days depending on the platform.

Step 4: Start Labeling

Begin accepting tasks. Start slow to learn the system. Quality matters more than speed!

Step 5: Get Paid

Payment usually comes weekly or biweekly. Choose your preferred payment method (PayPal, Payoneer, etc.)

Q: Do I need a website or business?

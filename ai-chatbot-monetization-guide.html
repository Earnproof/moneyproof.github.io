💬 AI Chatbot Kya Hai?

AI Chatbot ek computer program hai jo artificial intelligence use karke log se baat karta hai. ChatGPT, Claude, GPT-4 jaise models use karke ye questions ka jawab deta hai.

Samajhiye aasaan tarike se:

  • 24/7 customer support de sakta hai
  • Automatically questions ka jawab de sakta hai
  • Business ka kaam apne aap handle kar sakta hai
  • Lead qualify kar sakta hai
  • Suggestions de sakta hai
Simple Example: Ek business ne AI chatbot banaya apne customers ke liye. Customer ka sawaal poochta hai, chatbot automatically jawab deta hai. Business ke paise bachte hain, customer ko instant reply milta hai!

🤑 Kyu Chatbot Se Kumayein?

Bohot Demand Hai

Saray businesses AI chatbots chahte hain. Support costs kam karna hai unhe. Ye bohot bada market hai!

Easy Banane Ke Liye

Coding nahi aani zaruri. Make.com jaisi tools se koi bhi professional chatbot bana sakta hai 5 minutes mein!

Multiple Income Ways

Chatbot sell kar sakte ho, service de sakte ho, template bana kar sell kar sakte ho, ya course banana. Kayi tarike hain!

Scale Karna Asaan

Ek chatbot banao, 100 businesses ko sell karo. Pure scalability!

💰 5 Tarike Paise Kumane Ke

Method 1: Custom Chatbots Sell Karo

Kaise kaam karta hai:

  • Ek business dhundo jo chatbot chahta hai
  • Unke liye custom chatbot banao
  • $500-2000 charge karo
  • 1 sale per month = $500-2000 income!

Method 2: Fiverr/Upwork Par Service De

Kaise kaam karta hai:

  • Service package banao
  • Basic: $100-200
  • Pro: $300-500
  • Premium: $800-1500
  • 3-5 orders = $300-800/month!

Method 3: Templates Banao aur Sell Karo

Kaise kaam karta hai:

  • 5-10 reusable templates banao
  • Gumroad/Etsy par sell karo

  • 10-20 sales = $200-1000/month!

Method 4: Monthly Maintenance Karo

Kaise kaam karta hai:

  • Chatbot banao clients ke liye
  • Monthly maintenance fee lo
  • $100-300/month per client
  • 5 clients = $500-1500/month recurring!

Method 5: Course Banao

Kaise kaam karta hai:

  • "Chatbot Banao aur Sell Karo" course banao
  • Udemy/Teachable/Gumroad par sell karo
  • $30-100 per course

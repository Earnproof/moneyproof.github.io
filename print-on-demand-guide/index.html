1. What is Print on Demand?

Print on Demand (POD) is a business model where you create custom designs for products — t-shirts, mugs, phone cases, hoodies, wall art — and sell them online. When a customer orders, your POD supplier prints the design onto the product and ships it directly to the customer. You never hold inventory, never buy stock upfront, and never touch the product.

The global print on demand market is currently valued at $12.96 billion and is projected to reach $102.99 billion by 2034 — a 26% growth rate every year. This is not a saturated market. It is an exploding one.

💡
Why POD in 2026?

132,700 people search "print on demand" every month worldwide. Buyers want personalized, unique products. POD gives you exactly that — custom products made after the sale, zero waste, zero risk.

The complete POD flow: you design → customer orders → supplier prints & ships → you earn profit

2. POD vs Regular Dropshipping — Key Differences

Both models mean zero inventory and zero upfront cost — but they work differently:

ProductsCustom designed by youExisting products from suppliers
Uniqueness100% unique — your designsSame as competitors
Profit Margin30–60%15–35%
Shipping Time3–10 days (US/EU)7–30 days (AliExpress)
Brand BuildingYes — your brandHarder to brand
Startup CostFREEFREE
ℹ️
Which is Better?

POD has higher margins and uniqueness — your designs cannot be copied exactly. Dropshipping has faster testing cycles. Many sellers do both: test niches with dropshipping, build brands with POD.

3. Best Print on Demand Products in 2026

Not all POD products sell equally. These are the highest-demand, highest-margin products right now:

Top 6 POD products with the highest demand and profit margins in 2026

T-Shirts & Hoodies

The classic POD product. Hoodies generate an estimated 10,903 monthly sales on Amazon alone with around $268,497 in monthly revenue. Graphic designs, retro fonts, and niche memes sell extremely well. Best niches: pet lovers, gaming, motivational quotes, professions (nurses, teachers, engineers).

Custom Mugs

Consistently top-selling across Etsy and Amazon. "Custom mugs" gets 22,200 monthly searches. Affordable to produce, easy to design, perfect for gifting occasions. Profit margin: 40–60%.

Phone Cases

Average 68% net margin according to market data — one of the highest in POD. Everyone has a smartphone and replaces their case regularly. Custom phone cases average around $18,760 in monthly revenue on Amazon. Design for the top 5 iPhone and Samsung models.

Custom Stickers

"Custom stickers" reaches 110,000 estimated monthly searches. Cheap to produce, easy to ship worldwide, loved by students, laptop owners, and planners. Great for testing new design styles quickly.

Tote Bags & Wall Art

Home decor POD products are projected to grow 28% annually through 2034. Wall art posters, canvas prints, and tote bags are perfect for lifestyle brands targeting women aged 25–45.

✅
Beginner Recommendation

Start with mugs + t-shirts in one niche. Easy to design, fast to test, affordable for customers. Once you find a winning design, expand to hoodies and phone cases in the same niche.

5. Free Design Tools for POD

You do not need to be a designer to succeed in POD. These free tools make design simple:

Tool 1

Canva (Free)

The easiest design tool on the internet. Canva has thousands of t-shirt, mug, and poster templates. The free plan is more than enough for beginners. Use Canva to create your base design, then upload to Printify or Printful. Tip: Use transparent background PNG exports for apparel designs.

Tool 2

Adobe Express (Free)

Adobe's free design tool — slightly more professional than Canva for typography. Great for text-based designs like quotes and motivational prints. Exports high-resolution files perfect for POD printing.

Tool 3

Kittl (Free Plan)

Purpose-built for POD sellers. Kittl has vintage, retro, and professional design templates that look hand-crafted. The free plan gives 5 exports per month — perfect for testing new designs before committing.

⚠️
Copyright Warning

Never use trademarked phrases, logos, or celebrity names on your designs. No Disney characters, no sports team logos, no brand names. Platforms like Etsy and Amazon permanently ban accounts for copyright violations. Use original designs only.

6. Setting Up Your POD Store Step by Step

Step 1

Choose Your Niche

Pick one focused niche before designing anything. Best beginner niches: dog lovers, cat owners, teachers, nurses, gym motivation, hiking/outdoor, funny quotes for moms. The more specific the niche, the less competition and the higher the conversion rate.

Step 2

Create 5–10 Designs on Canva

Design 5–10 variations in your niche. Focus on text-based designs first — they are easiest to create and sell consistently. Example for "dog niche": "Dog Mom", "Rescue Dog Dad", "My Dog is My Therapist." Simple, relatable, proven to sell.

Step 3

Create a Printify Account

Go to printify.com and sign up free. Connect it to your Shopify store or Etsy shop. In Printify, click "Add Product" → choose product type → upload your design → position it → save.

Step 4

Set Up Your Store

For beginners: Etsy is easier to start — built-in audience, no marketing needed immediately. For full control and higher profits: Shopify. Many successful POD sellers run both simultaneously — Etsy for traffic, Shopify for brand building.

Step 5

Write SEO-Optimized Listings

Your product title and tags determine if customers find you. Use all available tags. Example title: "Funny Dog Mom Mug — Gift for Dog Lover — 11oz Ceramic Coffee Cup — Dog Owner Gift — Dog Mom Gift". Pack in keywords naturally — think like a buyer searching for a gift.

Pricing Psychology

Never price at round numbers. $24.99 outsells $25 consistently. Add "Gift" or "Limited Edition" to your title — it justifies a 20–30% higher price and increases perceived value.

8. Marketing Your POD Store for Free

Pinterest (Best Free Channel for POD)

Pinterest is the #1 free marketing platform for Print on Demand stores. POD products are visual — and Pinterest is a visual search engine. Create a business account, make boards for each product type, and pin your designs daily. Pinterest traffic builds slowly but can drive consistent free sales for years. Mugs, t-shirts, wall art, and tote bags perform exceptionally well.

TikTok Organic

Show your design process — how you go from a blank canvas to a finished product on Canva, then live in your Printify store. "Behind the scenes" POD content goes viral regularly. Unboxing your own sample orders gets millions of views. Post daily for 4–6 weeks and you will see results.

Instagram Reels & Stories

Lifestyle shots of your products perform extremely well. Order samples of your top 3 products, photograph them in real settings, and post Reels. Use product-specific hashtags like #doggifts #teachermugs #gymshirt.

Etsy SEO (Free Built-in Traffic)

Etsy has 500 million monthly visits. Optimize your listing titles and tags with buyer-intent keywords. Use eRank or EverBee (both have free plans) to find low-competition, high-search keywords on Etsy. Good SEO alone can bring you consistent daily sales.

9. Common POD Mistakes to Avoid

  • Too many niches at once — Focus on ONE niche for 60 days before expanding. Spreading too thin means mastering nothing.
  • Generic designs — "Dog Mom" is fine. "Golden Retriever Mom Who Drinks Coffee and Hates Mondays" is better. Be specific.
  • Not ordering samples — Order your bestselling design before pushing it heavily. Check print quality, color accuracy, and sizing yourself.
  • Ignoring SEO — A beautiful design buried on page 10 of Etsy makes zero sales. Keywords matter more than design quality for discoverability.
  • Quitting too early — Most POD stores take 30–90 days to make consistent sales. It is a slow build — but a durable one.

🌍 Worldwide & Pakistan — How to Run POD From Anywhere

Print on Demand is one of the most beginner-friendly businesses you can run from Pakistan or any country worldwide. Here is your complete local setup guide:

Which Platform to Sell On from Pakistan?

  • Etsy: Etsy is available in Pakistan — you can create a seller account and list products. However, Etsy Payments direct deposit is not available. Use Payoneer to receive Etsy payouts directly.
  • Shopify + Printify: Create your own store. Use PayPal as payment method — works in Pakistan, accepts customers from 200+ countries.
  • Amazon Merch: Invite-only in 2026. Not ideal for Pakistan-based beginners to start with.

Receiving Payments in Pakistan

  • Payoneer: Free US bank account. Etsy sends payouts here. Withdraw to your Pakistani bank in PKR. This is the most reliable method for Pakistan-based POD sellers.
  • PayPal: Works for Shopify stores. Customers worldwide can pay — you receive USD and withdraw via Payoneer linking.
  • Wise: Best exchange rate USD → PKR. Link to Etsy or Shopify payouts for maximum value on every withdrawal.

Target Western Buyers Always

Target US, UK, Canada, and Australia customers. They have higher purchasing power, use credit cards freely, and buy custom gifts regularly — especially for holidays, birthdays, and special occasions. Your POD supplier ships directly from their facility to your customer. You are based in Pakistan but running a global brand. That is the power of Print on Demand.

AI Labeling Kya Hai?

Seedhi baat karein to duniya ki badi badi technology companies jaise Google, Tesla, aur Amazon apni AI ko train karne ke liye data chahti hain. Is data ko insaan tayyar karta hai. Jaise kisi gaari ki tasveer mein yeh batana ke yahan pedestrian hai, yahan traffic light hai, ya yahan doosri gaari hai. Yahi kaam AI Labeling kehlata hai, aur is kaam ki demand din ba din barh rahi hai.

Aaj hum aapko 4 aese platforms ke baare mein batayenge jo Pakistan aur India mein kaam karte hain, jahan se aap ghar baith kar $100 se $300 tak kama sakte hain. Har platform ka poora safar aapko yahan milega — account banana se lekar jeb mein paise aane tak.

Koi experience zarori nahi. In tamam platforms par training bilkul free hai. Aapko sirf ek smartphone ya laptop aur stable internet chahiye.

Platform 01

Remotasks

Bilkul pehli baar shuruat karne walon ke liye sabse behtar platform. Scale AI ne banaya hai.

Kaam Kya Hota Hai?

2D Annotation

Kisi image mein objects ke aas paas boxes banate hain — gaariyon par, logon par aur signboards par alag alag boxes. Yeh sabse asaan kaam hai aur beginners ke liye ideal hai.

LiDAR — 3D Mapping

Self-driving cars ke sensor data ko label karna. Thora mushkil lekin zyada paying. Thodi practice ke baad seekha ja sakta hai.

Step-by-Step Shuruat Kaise Karein?

  • 01

    remotasks.com par jaakar bilkul free account banayein.

  • 02

    Account bante hi "Remotasks University" ka option milega — yeh free training section hai jahan chote videos hain.

  • 03

    Har course ke baad ek chota test hota hai — pass karo aur woh kaam aapke dashboard mein aa jata hai.

  • 04

    Ek ghante ki lagan se training mukammal karein aur pehla task uthayein.

Social Media Evaluation

Platform 03

TELUS International

Canadian company. Sabse reliable aur professional platform — ek baar qualify, mahine ki guaranteed income.

Kaam Kya Hota Hai?

Ads Rating

Google par aane wale advertisements ko rate karna — yeh ad us search se kitna relevant hai. Ise "Rater" ka kaam bhi kaha jata hai.

Maps Evaluation

Google Maps ki galtiyan theek karna — galat naam, galat address, galat category. Bahut safe aur legal kaam hai.

Step-by-Step Shuruat Kaise Karein?

  • 01

    TELUS International ki website par jaakar apne mulk ke mutabiq apply karein.

  • 02

    Company aapko ek "Guidelines Document" bhejti hai — takreeban 20-30 pages. Ise poori tafseel se parh lein.

  • 03

    Parhai ke baad ek Online Exam hota hai. Thora mushkil hai, lekin guidelines achhi tarah parh li hain to pass ho jata hai.

  • 04

    Exam pass — aur aap Qualified Rater ban jaate hain. Mahine ki pakki aamdani shuru!

Pro Tip: Guidelines document ko ek baar nahi, do baar parhen. Exam mein aksar wahi sawaal aate hain jo log ignore kar dete hain.

    Yeh zyada se zyada ek regular part-time job ki tarah hai — long-term aur stable.
